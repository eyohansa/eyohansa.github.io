I Watched Hellboy: The Crooked Man

Today, I went to watch Hellboy: The Crooked Man in theater. I hadn't heard about this movie so it was a surprise. I didn't watch any trailer so I went in blind. I was a fan of the first Hellboy and I have watched it numerous times.

Well, color me surprised. It wasn't like the first movie at all but in a good way. I'm actually not good for horror movies and I don't handle jumpscares well. This movie has a lot of the later. The style of this movie reminded me of a Tarantino movie (with intermezzo) but a horror one.

Personally, I quite liked it. I usually went to r/movies in Reddit to see discussion about the movie if I like one. Apparently, this title didn't have theater screening in the States so there was not any thread about it.

Hence why I decided to write about it here instead.
