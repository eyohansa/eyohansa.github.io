Building Railway

I have been playing Satisfactory recently, with a friend, ever since its 1.0 launch. I've bought the game a long time ago when it was on a discount but haven't had a chance to play it. The launch has given me motivation to push everything else aside and finally played the game.

I've been having a blast with the game. I've always enjoyed factory-building games; having previously played Factorio and Dyson Sphere Program (my personal favorite). I guess I enjoy seeing numbers go up. It also helps that resource nodes don't deplete in this game so it's actually possible to never tidy your factory; no matter how unsightly it becomes.

Today, I spent most of my time building the in-game railway. I built mine on top of water on the Northwestern area; going from a Caterium Ore node West of my spawn, up to the oil fields in the North. Building above water means placing a lot of foundations.

Building foundations over the span of vast areas requires a lot of resources. Usually, carrying this many resources would fill up a player's inventory. Thankfully, ever since the launch, Satisfactory provides player with really nifty dimensional storage.

It looks quite majestic once finished

Aside from the tedious task of laying down foundation, the setting up was actually quite straightforward. I even managed to make it runs autonomously. It periodically brings a batch of plastic (and eventually, rubber) to my outpost which would bring it over to my base with conveyor belts. It's definitely a beauty to behold.

Is it too late to say I quite like trains?
